ABOUT US

OUR MISSION
AND VALUE

Young people who have a casual dress sense, who ignore stereotypes and who want to feel good in whatever they are wearing.

Pull&Bear started up in 1991 with a clear international mission and with the intention of dressing young people who are engaged with their environment, who live in the community and relate to each other.

1991 The Pull&Bear chain of fashion stores was born, a result of a market segmentation strategy initiated by the Inditex Group.
1992 The ability to understand the concept of global fashion, along with an aggressive price policy, facilitated its international expansion from 1992 onwards, with the opening of their first stores in Portugal.
1993 Pull&Bear arrived to Greece and Malta, and quickly gained strong position on both markets.
1991 The first collection for women sees the light of the day. Xdye is also added to the Pull&Bear line, offering sportier and more technological garments, from the very beginning linked to the icons of 21st century youth
